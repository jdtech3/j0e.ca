RoboDog

A small-sized robot dog (approx. ankle height) based on the plans published and open-sourced by James Bruton, guided by his set of videos about the project. Exploring my budding interests in control systems and bio-inspired robotics!

Supported by a #RisingYouth grant from TakingITGlobal/Government of Canada.

SSB Demodulator

Building a single sideband demoulator with a team as part of a hardware design course, meant to integrate together to form a fully functioning radio transmitting/receiving in the 8-16 MHz (HF) band.

Plane n' Simple ✈️

A couple of friends learning aerospace by designing and building our own fixed-wing remote-controlled aircraft. Currently in detail design of our revision 0—a 2m wingspan plane with custom airframe and off-the-shelf electronics.
